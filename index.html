The northeastern corner of the Savoy Ballroom in Harlem, near the bandstand, was where the swing virtuosos like Frankie Manning and Shorty George Snowden would put their skills on display back in the 1930’s. They called it the Cool Cat Corner, after the talented “cats” who danced there. The Savoy may be gone but the spirit of the Cool Cat Corner lives on in Charlotte, NC -- the place where swing dancers come together and dance!
